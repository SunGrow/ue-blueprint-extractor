v2 improvements over v1:
  • All bezier paths — no geometric primitives. Every shape is hand-curved.
  • Radial/linear gradients on body, hat, and headlamp for depth
  • Organic pear-shaped head (wider at cheeks) instead of a circle
  • Dramatic 18° hat tilt with a dangling loose chin strap (hat is REALLY sliding)
  • Free ear has natural floppy curve with fold line, not a rigid ellipse
  • Trapped ear has just the tip visible — more comedy in the implication
  • Eyes have iris detail, double catchlights, upper lid shadow
  • Thicker determined brows with better inner-lowered angle
  • Plump nose with highlight instead of flat triangle
  • Proper arms with connected paths, not floating ellipses
  • Paw pads on the left hand
  • Pickaxe with wood grain, curved blade, and highlight
  • Overalls with bib, front pocket, brass buttons with cross-hatch
  • Ground shadow ellipse for grounding
  • Cheek fur tufts and subtle whiskers
  • Feet as organic shapes with proper connection to legs
  • Cotton tail with highlight behind body
